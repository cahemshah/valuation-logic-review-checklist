# Valuation Logic Review Checklist

This checklist reflects structured validation steps used while reviewing financial projections and DCF-based valuation outputs.

Key review checks include:

• Terminal growth rate must remain below discount rate  
• Discount rate must reflect business risk profile  
• Revenue growth assumptions should align with industry benchmarks  
• Capex assumptions must match expansion narrative  
• Working capital assumptions must scale consistently with revenue  
• Depreciation affects tax shield but not operating cash flow directly  
• Free cash flow reconciliation must align with projected financial statements  

These validation steps are useful when reviewing AI-generated financial modeling outputs for internal consistency and realism.
